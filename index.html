Traveling since a young age and from an unusual background , Lisa Deb reveals her many experiences through music. Deep within her soul lies many feelings which she is able to transmit directly to you, transporting your spirit on a ride of uplifting emotions.

Although born in Aix-en-provence with a Belgian nationality she is really a colourful mix of many cultures. Raised by missionaries she was constantly around music and would perform with her siblings around the globe from Madagascar to South America.
